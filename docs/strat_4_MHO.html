1.9 分層分析-4: Metabolically Healthy Obesity(MHO)

Criteria:

  1. Obesity: BMI ≥30 kg/m2

  2. Fasted serum triglycerides: ≤150 mg/dl

  3. HDL cholesterol serum concentrations:

    1. >40 mg/dl (in men)

    2. >50 mg/dl (in women)

  4. Blood Pressure:

    1. Systolic blood pressure (SBP) ≤130 mmHg

    2. Diastolic blood pressure ≤85 mmHg

  5. Fasting blood glucose ≤100 mg/dl

    Reference: Blüher M. Metabolically Healthy Obesity. Endocr Rev. 2020 May 1;41(3):bnaa004. doi: 10.1210/endrev/bnaa004. PMID: 32128581; PMCID: PMC7098708.
